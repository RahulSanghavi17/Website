About

Finding the books you desire can be difficult at times. Maybe they're out of print or they're too pricey. This is a unique programme that allows users to put adverts on their available books and see them based on their location (Target Barrie City - For Now). Users can post books they want to barter with books they already own. Students will be able to locate textbooks at reasonable costs, and readers will be able to buy/barter books that have been used with care by other readers.

Working

  • Has an animated splash screen that automatically redirects to the login page
  • User logs in with their username and password. User Registration using Social Media
  • Show all of the most recent adverts in a card format.
  • Filtering is available to the user. Search for posts by area and category.
  • View the post's details (Images, Price, Seller Contact Details)
  • Users can create new ads, edit existing ads, and delete existing ads.
  • All of the adverts that they have uploaded can be viewed by the user.
